Direct Transmission

Andreas Handel

2020-08-02

Overview

In this module, we will discuss different types of transmission and then focus on direct transmission, and how this affects ID dynamics.

Learning Outcomes

In this module, you will learn to:

  • Assess how different modes of direct transmission affect ID dynamics.

  • Contrast the difference between density- and frequency-dependent transmission.

  • Predict how population size impacts ID dynamics for different types of transmission.

Assignments

  • The “Types of infectious disease transmission” chapter of the DSAIDE book.
  • The “Modes of Direct Transmission” chapter of the DSAIDE book.
  • The paper “How should pathogen transmission be modelled?” by McCallum et al. 2001 Trends in Ecology and Evolution

##Videos to watch In this video from the “Epidemics MOOC”, Marcel Salathe provides an overview of different types of ID transmission.
